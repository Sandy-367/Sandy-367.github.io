How To Succeed In Adulting

Adulting Definition: Behaving in a responsible and grown-up way. Able to manage mundane tasks, such as paying bills, cooking dinner, and running errands. Taking care of all these things confidently.

Examples of Adulting:

  • Taking care of laundry
  • Keeping the house clean
  • Buying groceries, cooking meals
  • Paying bills, Managing Personal finances
  • Healthcare - doctor's visits, prescriptions
  • Car maintenance - oil change, tire pressure, registration renewal
  • Mail - going to the post office AND reading/responding to email
  • Maintenance & Decor - hanging a picture, using a drill to hang a shelf
